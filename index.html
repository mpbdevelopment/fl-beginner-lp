Common questions

Do I need any experience?

None at all. Our beginner clinics are designed for people who have never picked up a paddle. Most of our players started exactly where you are.

Do I need to bring equipment?

We provide everything. Just show up in comfortable athletic clothing and sneakers.

What if I'm not very athletic?

Pickleball is for everyone. Our coaches adapt to your level, and our beginner groups are welcoming and low-pressure. That's why people love it.

How big are the groups?

Small. You'll get plenty of personal attention from our coaches. It's one of the things our players mention most in their reviews.
