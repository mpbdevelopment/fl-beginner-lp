Common Questions

Do I need any experience?

None at all. These clinics are designed specifically for people who have never picked up a paddle. Our coaches start from scratch and move at your pace.

Do I need to bring equipment?

Nope. We provide paddles and balls for every session. Just wear comfortable athletic shoes and clothes you can move in.

What if I’m not athletic?

Pickleball is one of the most accessible sports out there. The court is small, the ball moves at a manageable pace, and our coaches focus on technique over athleticism. Players of all fitness levels thrive in our clinics.

How big are the groups?

We keep groups small so every player gets personal attention from the coach. You won’t get lost in a crowd.
